The origins of cricket

    Early Cricket

    The History of Cricket by Ben Johnson via Historic UK
    Cricket On The Artillery Ground via ICC Cricket
  • Cricket was created during the era of the Anglo-Saxans and Normans, but the true reference of the origins of the sport comes prior to the start of the 17th century.
  • There have been speculations that cricket was a game primarily meant for children before adults started playing it. During the early days, balls and bats weren't available so players typically used stones or small pieces of wood as the ball, farm tools as the bat, and tree trunks as the wickets.
  • The first ever reference to cricket being meant as a sport for adults occurred in 1611 when a couple of people were arrested for playing cricket when they instead, should've attended church.
